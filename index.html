About Flo House Studio

Flo House Studio is a Nashville-based mixing, mastering, and production studio dedicated to helping artists bring their vision to life with professional-quality sound.


Founded by Alyssa Marie Coon and Sebastian Belen, Flo House Studio combines technical engineering expertise with real-world experience as musicians, producers, and songwriters. Together, they have attended respected Nashville institutions for audio engineering and music production, developing the skills and industry knowledge needed to compete at a professional level.


Projects associated with our team have been featured in major publications including Rolling Stone, AP, and #1 on the top leading indie chart aBreak 58..


We work with artists across all genres, from country and pop to rock, rap, singer-songwriter, and beyond. Whether you're looking for a polished mix, a release-ready master, or full production support, we're committed to delivering results that help your music stand out.


🎵 First-time clients receive discounted rates. Contact us to learn more and get started on your next project.
