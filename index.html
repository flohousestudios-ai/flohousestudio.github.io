About Us

Flo House Studio is a Nashville-based recording, mixing, and mastering studio serving artists of all genres.

Our work has contributed to music featured in major publications including Rolling Stone and the Associated Press.

We offer discounted rates for first-time clients because we believe great music deserves a chance to be heard.

Meet the Owners

Sebastian Belen and Alyssa Marie Coon are engineers, producers, and musicians dedicated to helping artists create professional records that stand out.

With backgrounds in music production and performance, Flo House Studio combines technical expertise with an artist-first approach.
